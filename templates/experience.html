FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods are accepted?

Hello Airport Hotel accepts cash, bank transfers, and all major credit cards, ensuring convenient payment options for our guests.

How do I cancel or modify my booking?

To cancel or modify your booking, log in to your account on our website and navigate to the "My Bookings" section. Alternatively, you can contact our support team for assistance.

Do you provide airport shuttle services?

Yes, we provide airport shuttle services for our guests. Please provide your flight details during booking to arrange this service.

What amenities are included in the rooms?

All rooms at Hello Airport Hotel are equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, air conditioning,Hot water showers, TVs, and 24-hour room service for your comfort.

Are there any discounts for long-term stays?

Yes, we offer special discounts for guests staying more than a week. Contact our reservation team to learn about long-term stay packages.
